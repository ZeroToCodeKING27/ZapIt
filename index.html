Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about pest control, inspections, and prevention so you can protect your home with confidence.

1. What types of pests do you handle?

We handle a wide range of pests, including ants, termites, cockroaches, rodents, bed bugs, spiders, wasps, and more. If you have a specific concern, our experts can provide a tailored solution.

2. Are your treatments safe for children and pets?

Yes, our treatments are designed to be safe for children and pets. We use environmentally friendly products and follow strict safety guidelines to ensure the well-being of all family members.

3. Can you prevent pests, or only remove them?

We specialize in both prevention and removal. Our team identifies vulnerabilities, seals entry points, and applies preventative treatments to keep pests from returning.

4. How quickly will I see results?

Some pests, like ants or roaches, may respond within a few days, while more stubborn infestations, like termites or rodents, may require multiple visits. We’ll provide a timeline specific to your situation.

5. Do I need to prepare my home before a pest treatment?

Yes, depending on the treatment. Typically, we recommend clearing areas, covering food, and temporarily moving pets. Our technician will give detailed preparation instructions before every visit.
